Neutralizing Narcissism

Pattern literacy for a safer, saner web — and a kinder world.

We teach people to recognize manipulative language patterns, set clean boundaries, and build cultures where truth has oxygen. This page shares our purpose, method, and the ethic behind the work.

Mission

To expose coercive narcissistic patterns at the level of language, empower survivors with tools that restore agency, and prototype accountability systems that function without mobbing or dehumanization.

Fieldnote-driven • Survivor-centered • Research-informed

What we do

  • Pattern Literacy: plain-language explainers that help you spot tactics early—love-bombing, DARVO, triangulation, erasure.
  • Forensic Copy Reads: we analyze public posts or messages for red flags (no doxxing, no harassment).
  • Fieldnotes & Essays: mythic + practical writing that translates research into lived clarity.
  • Design for Accountability: architectures that make truth breathable—documentation flows, consented receipts, and culture-level hygiene.

Core Principles

  • Witness over War: illumination is not accusation; we show patterns, we don’t hunt people.
  • Boundaries are Love: clarity is care—for self, community, and even for those still trapped in shame loops.
  • Receipts, not Rage: timelines and texts out-perform arguments. Document. Date. Context.
  • Design the Atmosphere: build rooms where lies cannot breathe: clear rules, slow thinking, mutual consent.

Patterns We Expose

  • Erasure & revisionism
  • DARVO & gaslighting
  • Pathological mirroring & faux-empathy
  • Triangulation and manufactured fog
  • Grandiosity veneer; victim core

How We Work

We publish iterative fieldnotes and guides, each one designed to be shared, taught, and remixed. Our cadence favors clarity over virality, and repair over spectacle. When we analyze public writing, we address patterns, not identities.

When communities ask for help, we co-design simple hygiene practices—calm channels, consented archiving, and de-escalation scripts.

Ethics & Safety

  • No brigading, no stalking, no pile-ons.
  • We never publish private information or contact details.
  • We respect platform rules and local law.
  • We support trauma-informed moderation and referral to licensed care when appropriate.
